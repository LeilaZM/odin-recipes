French Toast

Not quite toast, not quite a pancake - great for using up older bread!

Very quick and easy french toast

Ingredients

serves six
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 6 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients

    2. Mix beaten eggs, sugar, milk, cinnamon, and salt in a large, shallow bowl.

  2. Dip bread

    3. Dip bread slices into egg mixture to coat.

  3. Cook bread

    4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook bread in hot skillet until browned completely, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
